Well-Known Tennis Players

Roger Federer

Roger Federer is a Swiss professional tennis player, widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the sport.

Serena Williams

Serena Williams is an American tennis player who is regarded as one of the greatest female athletes in history.

Rafael Nadal

Rafael Nadal is a Spanish professional tennis player, known as the "King of Clay" for his dominance on the surface.

Roger Federer Achievements
  • 20 Grand Slam titles
  • 310 weeks at World No. 1
  • 8 Wimbledon titles
Serena Williams Achievements
  • 23 Grand Slam singles titles (most by any player in the Open Era)
  • 4 Olympic gold medals (1 in singles, 3 in doubles)
  • 7 Wimbledon titles
  • Fastest woman to serve at 128.6 mph
Rafael Nadal Achievements
  • 22 Grand Slam singles titles
  • 14 French Open titles (most in history)
  • 2 Olympic gold medals (1 in singles, 1 in doubles)
  • Over 1,000 career wins

Tennis Rules

Tennis is played with a ball and racket. It involves two players (singles) or two teams of two players each (doubles)...

  • A tennis match is played in sets.
  • Each set consists of games, where a player serves.
  • The objective is to win points by hitting the ball over the net and into the opponent's court.
