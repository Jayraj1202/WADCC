How to Reach Jog Falls
Jog Falls is located in Shivamogga, Karnataka. It is well connected by road and train. The nearest airport is Hubli.
How to Reach Dudhsagar Falls
Take a train to Kulem, then travel by jeep or trek to the falls. Best visited during the monsoon season.
How to Reach Bhimlat Falls
Reach Bundi by train or bus, then take local transport to Bhimlat Falls.
